Shaaby

Note: Shaaby is a form of belly dance that originated in Egypt. It is a modern and energetic style of dance that has become very popular in recent years.

Some of the characteristics of Shaaby belly dance include:

Fast-paced movements

Shaaby belly dance is known for its fast and energetic movements, which require a lot of stamina and agility.

Hip-focused movements

Shaaby belly dance is characterized by hip-focused movements, such as hip drops, shimmies, and rotations.

 

Incorporation of other dance styles

Shaaby belly dance often incorporates elements from other dance styles, such as hip hop, salsa, and reggaeton.

Playful and fun

The dance style is often performed in a playful and fun manner, with dancers interacting with the audience and encouraging them to join in.

Street-style influence

The dance style is heavily influenced by street-style movements and has a raw and unpolished feel to it.
