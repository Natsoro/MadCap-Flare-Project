Classical Egyptian dance

This is an example of a very elegant classical dance. It is characterized by significance and emotional saturation. We can see many transitions and emotions in the dancer's body.

Folklore

Saidi - a dance with a cane (assaya). We can see a dancer wearing a long dress covering most of the body (galabiya).

Baladi - a dance with many slow movements mixed with the sharp ones. The music contains accordion chords and drum(darbuka) rythmes.

Khaleegy - typically involves a lot of hands, shoulder and head movements. Often the dancer's hair is involved.

Dance fusions

Tribal Fusion - what distinguishes it from the classic belly dance is a strong attitude taken from flamenco and very smooth movements. The nature of the dance is dark and mysterious.

American Tribal Style (ATS) - the oldest variant of the tribal style, consisting in performing group improvisation.
